# 🏠 Tenant User Guide - DamianixPro

Welcome to DamianixPro! This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your tenant experience.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Go to the DamianixPro website
   - Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
   - Select "Tenant" as your role

2. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Fill in your personal information
   - Upload a profile photo
   - Add your contact details
   - Verify your email address

3. **Document Verification**
   - Upload required documents (ID, proof of income)
   - Wait for verification (usually 24-48 hours)
   - Receive confirmation email

## 🏡 Finding Properties

### Property Search

1. **Use the Search Filters**
   - Location: Select your preferred area
   - Price Range: Set your budget (in Nigerian Naira ₦)
   - Property Type: Apartment, House, or Commercial
   - Bedrooms/Bathrooms: Specify your needs
   - Amenities: Pool, Gym, Parking, etc.

2. **Browse Property Listings**
   - View high-quality photos
   - Read detailed descriptions
   - Check amenities and features
   - See location on interactive map

3. **Save Favorites**
   - Click the heart icon to save properties
   - Access saved properties from your dashboard
   - Compare multiple properties easily

### Property Details

Each property listing includes:

- **Photos & Virtual Tours**: High-quality images and 360° views
- **Detailed Description**: Property features and highlights
- **Amenities**: Available facilities and services
- **Location**: Interactive map and neighborhood info
- **Pricing**: Rent, deposit, and additional fees
- **Contact Info**: Owner/Agent contact details

## 📋 Application Process

### Submitting Applications

1. **Click "Apply Now"** on your chosen property
2. **Complete Application Form**
   - Personal information
   - Employment details
   - References (previous landlords, employers)
   - Preferred move-in date

3. **Upload Required Documents**
   - Valid ID (National ID, Driver's License, Passport)
   - Proof of income (salary slips, bank statements)
   - Employment letter
   - Previous tenancy references

4. **Submit Application**
   - Review all information
   - Pay application fee (if applicable)
   - Submit for review

### Application Status

Track your applications in your dashboard:

- **Pending**: Under review by owner/agent
- **Approved**: Congratulations! Proceed to lease signing
- **Rejected**: Unfortunately not selected
- **Withdrawn**: You cancelled the application

## 💳 Payment Management

### Making Payments

1. **Access Payment Dashboard**
   - Go to "Payments" tab in your dashboard
   - View all payment obligations
   - See payment history

2. **Payment Methods Available**
   - **Paystack**: Credit/Debit cards, Bank transfer, USSD
   - **Flutterwave**: Alternative payment methods
   - **Direct Bank Transfer**: Transfer to provided account
   - **USSD Codes**: Mobile banking (*737#, *919#, etc.)

3. **Making a Payment**
   - Click "Make Payment" on pending payment
   - Select payment method
   - Enter amount (pre-filled for rent)
   - Complete secure payment process
   - Download receipt automatically

### Payment Types

- **Rent**: Monthly rental payments
- **Deposit**: Security deposit (usually 1-2 months rent)
- **Agent Fee**: Real estate agent commission
- **Legal Fee**: Documentation and agreement fees
- **Utility Bills**: Electricity, water, internet (if applicable)

### Payment Reminders

- Automatic email reminders 7 days before due date
- SMS notifications for urgent payments
- Late payment alerts and fees
- Payment confirmation receipts

## 🔧 Maintenance Requests

### Submitting Maintenance Requests

1. **Go to Maintenance Tab**
   - Access from your dashboard
   - Click "Submit New Request"

2. **Fill Request Details**
   - **Title**: Brief description of issue
   - **Category**: Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC, General
   - **Priority**: Low, Medium, High, Urgent
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
   - **Photos**: Upload images if helpful

3. **Submit Request**
   - Review information
   - Submit to property manager
   - Receive confirmation number

### Tracking Requests

Monitor your maintenance requests:

- **Submitted**: Request received and logged
- **Assigned**: Technician/vendor assigned
- **In Progress**: Work has begun
- **Completed**: Issue resolved
- **Closed**: Request closed with your approval

### Emergency Requests

For urgent issues (water leaks, electrical problems, security):

- Mark priority as "Urgent"
- Call emergency contact number
- Submit online request for documentation

## 📄 Document Management

### Document Types

Keep these documents updated in your profile:

- **Identity Documents**: National ID, Passport, Driver's License
- **Income Proof**: Salary slips, bank statements, employment letter
- **Lease Agreement**: Signed rental agreement
- **Payment Receipts**: All payment confirmations
- **Insurance**: Tenant insurance policy (if applicable)

### Uploading Documents

1. **Go to Documents Tab**
2. **Click "Upload Document"**
3. **Select Document Type**
4. **Choose File** (PDF, JPG, PNG accepted)
5. **Add Description** (optional)
6. **Upload and Save**

### Document Security

- All documents are encrypted and secure
- Only you and authorized personnel can access
- Regular backups ensure data safety
- Download copies anytime

## 🏠 Lease Management

### Understanding Your Lease

Your lease agreement includes:

- **Property Details**: Address, description, condition
- **Lease Term**: Start date, end date, renewal options
- **Rent Amount**: Monthly rent, payment due date
- **Deposit**: Security deposit amount and conditions
- **Rules**: Property rules and tenant responsibilities
- **Termination**: Notice period and conditions

### Lease Renewal

2 months before lease expiry:

- Receive renewal notification
- Review new terms and rent amount
- Accept or decline renewal
- Sign new lease agreement if accepting

### Moving Out

When ready to move out:

1. **Give Notice**: Provide required notice period (usually 30-60 days)
2. **Schedule Inspection**: Property condition assessment
3. **Final Payments**: Clear any outstanding amounts
4. **Return Keys**: Hand over all keys and access cards
5. **Deposit Return**: Receive security deposit (minus deductions)

- Check email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" link
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser

- Check bank account balance
- Verify card details

**Document Upload Issues?**

- Use supported formats (PDF, JPG, PNG)
- Ensure stable internet connection
